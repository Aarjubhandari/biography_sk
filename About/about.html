She is so bold and strong and the girl who never scared of taking any challenges during her journey of life. Her each and every words inspires me to be bold and independent girl. In any situation, either it would be complex or easy, she tackle it by her confidence and smile. So, these are the reasons why i got motived by shrinkhala khadiwada.
